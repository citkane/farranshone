Farranshone Local Planning Steering Group

This group is concerned with the current and ongoing urban planning matters affecting our neighbourhood.

We are all local residents, and actively engaged in dialogue with our Metro North Councillors.

We continue to attempt to actively dialogue with Limerick Active Travel on behalf of our neighbourhood to advance orderly and progressive urban planning.

We regard our neighbourhood to be the roughly 500 residences bounded by Ennis Rd, Shelbourne Rd, High Rd, Bellefield Trc and Bellefield Gdns including Rockspring and Shelbourne Gardens

We apologise if your locality is not technically named "Farranshone", but this is used for convenience as the most central name to cover the area affected by current planning matters.

  • you are a local resident
  • You are eligible to vote
  • you are willing to have your name listed here

If you just wish to be kept informed of updates, please email the subject line "subsribe" to updates@farranshone.ie

This group was formed around a consensus of how we would like to see our neighbourhood planned and developed. You can download it here.

This consensus, signed by 216 of us, was handed to our Metro North Councillors as part of the recent Filtered Permeability Trial consultation.
