Farranshone Local Planning Steering Group

This group is concerned with the current and ongoing urban planning matters affecting our neighbourhood in Limerick City, Ireland.

We are local residents who, as a group, are engaging in dialogue with our Metro North Councillors.

We continue to attempt to actively dialogue with Limerick Active Travel on behalf of our neighbourhood to advance orderly and progressive urban planning.

We regard our neighbourhood to be the roughly 500 residences bounded by Ennis Rd, Shelbourne Rd, High Rd, Bellefield Trc and Bellefield Gdns including Rockspring and Shelbourne Gardens. We apologise if your locality is not technically named "Farranshone", but this is used for convenience as the most central name to cover the area affected by current planning matters.

If you just wish to be kept informed, please email the subject line "subscribe" to updates@farranshone.ie

This group was formed around a consensus of how we would like our neighbourhood to be planned and developed. You can download the pro-forma consensus here.

It was signed by 216 local residents and handed to our Metro North Councillors as part of the recent Filtered Permeability Trial consultation.

Apologies if you have not seen it, or nobody called to your door. We went to as many doors as possible in the very short time period we had available during the consultation. It is never too late to agree, sign it and send it to our local councillors.
